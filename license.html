To play the "Pay to Play" missions you need to have both a License Key and a Mission Key: the former uniquely identifies your system and is used by us to create a valid Mission Key, that in turn unlocks a single mission or campaign package.
1

Install the license program and determine yout System Unique ID Key (SUID). Consult our license program installation guide if you require guidance.
2

Make your purchase for any of the missions or campaign packages from the mission page. Only if this is your first purchase, send us an email at mapleflagmissions@gmail.com listing your name, email address, and the SUID Key. We will send you the Mission Key to your email account once the transaction has been processed.
3

Use the provided Mission Key to unlock and install the missions. Consult our mission installation guide if you require guidance.
